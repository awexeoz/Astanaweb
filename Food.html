ROKU

An establishment with a cool atmosphere in the spirit of Wong Kar-wai's films - dim lights, neons and that's all. The ceiling hung with red lanterns is a bright detail of the interior, but the only one. There is also a long bar counter and a neon sign with hieroglyphs that make an impression.

Instagram

GASTERIA

A restaurant with two branches, designed in European style - restrained but elegant. Sand colors, natural materials and a lot of light are the philosophy of the establishment. Here, by the way, not only the interior is beautiful, but also the presentation, as well as the names of the dishes.

Instagram

MEATADOR

In addition to the beautiful interior, which contains a lot of leather, wood and metal, the establishment has a cool feature - tables with a built-in brazier. You can fry the meat yourself, and then enjoy the juicy meat in the heat. A hood is installed above each such table. The chef also puts on a show with food served.
